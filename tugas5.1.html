Determinan Matriks 4x4 dengan Ekpansi Kofaktor#

1. langkah pertama buat matriksnya terlebih dahulu dengan ordo 4x4

\(\begin{matrix} A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 3 & 4 & 5\\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 1\\ 3 & 2 & 2 & 2\\ 3 & 2 & 5 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)

2. selanjutnya tulis rumus determinan disini, mengambil baris ke-2

\( det(A) = a_{21} c_{21} + a_{22} c_{22} + a_{23} c_{23} + a_{24} c_{24} \)

3. selanjutnya tentukan kofaktor nya disini dari matriks A

\(c_{21} = (-1)^{2+1} det(M_{21})\)
\(c_{22} = (-1)^{2+2} det(M_{22})\)
\(c_{23} = (-1)^{2+3} det(M_{23})\)
\(c_{24} = (-1)^{2+4} det(M_{24})\)

4. setelah itu kita akan cari satu-persatu determinan minor tersebut dari matriks A

5. pertama : \(M_{21}\)

\(\begin{matrix} det(M21) = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 4 & 5 \\ 2 & 2 & 2 \\ 2 & 5 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)

selanjutnya tulis rumus determinan, dan mengambil baris ke-2
\(det(M21) = a_{21}c_{21} + a_{22}c_{22} + a_{23}c_{23}\)

selanjutnya tentukan kofaktor dari matriks yang \(M_{21}, M_{22}, M_{23}\)

  • \(c_{21} = (-1)^{2 + 1} det(KM21)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM21) = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ 5 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)
\(det(KM21) = 4.4 - 5.5 = 16 - 25 = -9\)

hasil \(c_{21} = -1*-9\)

  • \(c_{22} = (-1)^{2+2}det(KM22)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM22) = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)
\(det(KM22) = 3.4 - 2.5 = 12 - 10 = 2\)

hasil \(c_{22} = -1*2\)

  • \(c_{23} = (-1)^{2+3}det(KM23)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM23) = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 4 \\ 2 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)
\(det(KM23) = 3.5 - 2.4 = 15 - 8 = 7\)

hasil \(c_{23} = -1*7\)

hasil akhir dari \(M_{21}\)
\(det(M21) = a_{21}c_{21} + a_{22}c_{22} + a_{23}c_{23}\)
\(det(M21) = 2.(-1).(-9) + 2.1.2 + 2.(-1).7\)
\(det(M21) = 18 + 4 - 14 = 8\)

dibuktikan dengan menggunakan numpy python

import numpy as np
M21 = np.array([[3,4,5], [2,2,2], [2,5,4]])
detM21 = np.linalg.det(M21)
print(round(detM21))

8

7. ketiga : \(M_{23}\)

selanjutnya tulis rumus determinan, dan mengambil baris ke-2
\(det(M21) = a_{21}c_{21} + a_{22}c_{22} + a_{23}c_{23}\)

selanjutnya tentukan kofaktor dari matriks yang \(M_{21}, M_{22}, M_{23}\)

\(det(KM21) = 2.4 - 2.5 = 8 - 10 = -2\)

hasil \(c_{21} = -1*-2\)

8. keempat : \(M_{24}\)

\(\begin{matrix} det(M24) = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 3 & 4 \\ 3 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 2 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)

selanjutnya tulis rumus determinan, dan mengambil baris ke-2
\(det(M21) = a_{21}c_{21} + a_{22}c_{22} + a_{23}c_{23}\)

selanjutnya tentukan kofaktor dari matriks yang \(M_{21}, M_{22}, M_{23}\)

  • \(c_{21} = (-1)^{2 + 1} det(KM21)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM21) = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 4 \\ 2 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)
\(det(KM21) = 3.5- 2.4 = 15 - 8 = 7\)

hasil \(c_{21} = -1*7\)

  • \(c_{22} = (-1)^{2+2}det(KM22)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM22) = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 4 \\ 3 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)
\(det(KM22) = 2.5 - 3.4 = 10 - 12 = -2\)

hasil \(c_{22} = -1*-2\)

  • \(c_{23} = (-1)^{2+3}det(KM23)\)

\( \begin{matrix} det(KM23) = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 3 \\ 3 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \end{matrix} \)

hasil \(c_{23} = -1*-5\)

hasil akhir dari \(M_{24}\)

9. langkah terakhir mencari determinan A

\(det(M_{21}) = 8\)
\(det(M_{22}) = 17\)
\(det(M_{23}) = 0\)
\(det(M_{24}) = -15\)

selanjutnya subtitusi nilai determinan dari matriks minornya

\(c_{21} = (-1)^{2+1}det(M_{21}) = -1.8\)
\(c_{22} = (-1)^{2+1}det(M_{22}) = 1.17\)
\(c_{23} = (-1)^{2+1}det(M_{23}) = -1.0\)
\(c_{24} = (-1)^{2+1}det(M_{24}) = -1.-15\)

\(a_{21}c_{21} + a_{22}c_{22} + a_{23}c_{23} + a_{24}c_{24} =\) \(1.(-1).8 + 1.1.17 + 1.(-1).0 + 1.(-1).(-15) =\) \(-8 + 17 + 0 + 15 = 4\)

import numpy as np
A = np.array([[2,3,4,5], [1,1,1,1], [3,2,2,2], [3,2,5,4]])
detA = np.linalg.det(A)
print("A: \n", A)
print()
print("Determinan A = ", round(detA))
